Welcome to OVDSaaS End to end Online Reproducible Apps Project


VALUING REPRODUCIBLE RESEARCH AT THE SERVICE OF THE INDUSTRY Creating synergies between scientists and industry, Solving cutting edge problems matching computational scientific research approach, editorial approach, available reproducibility technology approach and business approach

This Documentation explains you the end to end flow Reproducible Scientific Apps Creation. And the process can split into two parts.. In 1st module we majorly deals with Survey of business requirements of the target industry, develop MLops model. Whereas in second module we focus our journey towards develop custom Online web app (API) and integrate our machine learning model reproducible results.



Understand the problem, the business logic, the roles involved and segment it into microservices in BPML processes workflows

  2. Development of scientific research around the identified problems (scientist, authors)
  3. publication of results and reproducible artifacts with persistent identifiers, perennial DOIschain (publisher)
